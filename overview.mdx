## Features

- **Diverse Models**: Access multiple AI models optimized for different tasks and use cases
- **Multimodal Support**: Seamlessly combine text, images, and video in your interactions
- **Private Threads**: Secure, personalized conversations with AI models
- **Group Collaboration**: Invite others to participate in private threads
- **Media Generation**: Create images, videos, and sound effects from text descriptions
- **Voice Agent**: Engage in voice interactions with AI models
- **Transcript Agent**: Transcribe audio from media files

StreamPod provides a powerful interface for interacting with various AI models through the <a href="https://streampod.io/discord">Discord Server</a>. This guide will help you get started with text, image, and video generation capabilities.
